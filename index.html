What /teach does

It treats the working directory as a stateful learning workspace that persists across sessions, anchored to a single mission — the real reason the learner cares. Deep learning, it argues, needs three things:

KnowledgeFrom high-trust sources, never the model's memory.
SkillsAcquired through tight, interactive feedback loops.
WisdomTested against real practitioners & communities.
